Investor Education

Build your foundation

Everything you need before your first trade.

📈 What is a stock?

Buying a stock means buying a small piece of ownership in a company. If the company grows and earns more profit, your piece becomes worth more.

💡 Like owning a slice of a business — if the shop gets popular, your slice is worth more.

📊 RSI — Momentum

Relative Strength Index measures how fast prices are moving. Scale of 0–100. Tells you whether a stock is being overbought or oversold by traders.

💡 Above 70 = possibly overbought (may drop). Below 30 = possibly oversold (may bounce).

📉 Moving Averages

SMA smooths out daily price noise to show the real trend. The 20-day shows short-term momentum. The 50-day shows medium-term direction.

💡 Price crossing above SMA 50 is often a bullish signal. Crossing below is bearish.

🥚 Diversification

Spreading your money across different companies and sectors reduces risk. If one stock crashes, your whole portfolio doesn't go with it.

💡 A single index fund like VOO gives you 500 companies at once — instant diversification.

⏳ Why starting early matters — Compound interest

Your returns earn more returns over time. Starting at 16 vs 26 can mean the difference of hundreds of thousands of dollars by retirement — same money, just more time.

📖 Financial terms — hover any for a plain English explanation

P/E RATIO
Price vs earnings — how expensive the stock is relative to profits. Average is ~20-25x.
MARKET CAP
Total value of all shares. Small cap <$2B, Mid cap $2-10B, Large cap >$10B.
BETA
How much the stock moves vs the market. Beta of 2 means it moves 2x as much.
EPS
Earnings Per Share — how much profit the company makes per share of stock.
DIVIDEND
Cash paid to you just for holding the stock — like rent income from owning property.
52-WEEK RANGE
The highest and lowest price over the past year — shows how volatile the stock is.
BULL MARKET
Prices rising 20%+ from a recent low. Everyone is optimistic. Good time to be invested.
BEAR MARKET
Prices falling 20%+ from a recent high. Fear is high. Historically temporary.
